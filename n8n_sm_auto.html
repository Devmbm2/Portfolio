Automated Content Idea Generator & Social Media Poster with Approvals in n8n

This project demonstrates how to fully automate your content pipeline using n8n. It automatically generates fresh content ideas, adds them to Google Sheets, creates matching AI-generated images, posts to all social media channels in a cycle, sends an approval email to you, and emails the result to your client.

Project Objective

The goal is to run a hands-free social media system that consistently generates engaging, image-supported posts while maintaining your oversight through approval workflows before posting.

Workflow Overview

  • Auto-generate content ideas using OpenAI or Gemini
  • Store generated ideas in Google Sheets for tracking
  • Create an AI-generated image matching the post idea
  • Send an approval email to your email before posting
  • Once approved, post to all connected social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, X, etc.)
  • Send a summary result email with post links to the client
  • Cycle to the next idea and repeat automatically

Key Features

  • Content idea generation using AI
  • Google Sheets integration for idea management and tracking
  • AI-generated images for each post
  • Approval workflow via email before publishing
  • Automated posting to multiple social media channels
  • Result reporting to clients automatically via email
  • Completely no-code, scalable automation using n8n

Use Cases

  • Agencies managing multiple client social media accounts
  • Small businesses wanting consistent online presence
  • Personal brands aiming for regular, high-quality posts
