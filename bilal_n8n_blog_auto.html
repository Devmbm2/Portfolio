Automated Blog & Social Media Content Creator with Google Sheets and n8n

In this project, I demonstrate how to build an end-to-end content automation workflow in n8n. It automatically pulls a topic from a Google Sheets file, generates an SEO-friendly blog article with matching AI-generated images, and publishes the content to a website and social media platforms.

Project Objective

The goal is to fully automate your content pipeline: plan topics in Google Sheets, let AI generate high-quality blog posts with optimized images, and publish them everywhere—hands free.

Workflow Overview

  • Read new topics from Google Sheets
  • Use OpenAI to generate a complete SEO-friendly article
  • Create an AI-generated cover image to match the topic
  • Upload the article to your website CMS (WordPress, Webflow, etc.)
  • Share the same article and image to social media channels
  • Mark the topic as published in Google Sheets to avoid duplication

Key Features

  • Topic planning in Google Sheets
  • SEO-optimized text generation with OpenAI
  • Automatic AI image generation
  • Auto-publishing to website and social platforms
  • Fully automated, no-code flow using n8n

Use Cases

  • Small businesses that need regular blog content
  • Social media managers who want to automate posting
  • Personal bloggers and digital marketers

Results

With this setup, you can keep your website and social channels fresh with new, SEO-friendly articles and images—generated and posted automatically—saving hours every week.
